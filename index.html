About Us

Located in his parents' basement, Marcello’s Marvelous Music is a retro haven for sonic voyagers. It’s part vinyl shop, part rehearsal space, part recording studio. Whether you’re hunting for obscure records, jamming with your band, or tracking your next masterpiece — we’ve got the gear, the vibes, and fresh food straight from the roomies.

  • Used vinyl record sales
  • Rehearsal space for bands of all styles
  • Adaptive recording studio; producing, engineering, mixing, and mastering for any style
