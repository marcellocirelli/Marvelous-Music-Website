Our Services

Marcello's Marvelous Music isn’t just a record shop — it's a basement-built creative ecosystem, powered by passion, vintage gear, and more cables than there should be.

What we've got:

  • Used Vinyl Sales: A curated selection of obscure prog, fusion, avant-garde, and underground gems. If you've never heard of it, we probably have it.
  • Rehearsal Space: Fully equipped with quality gear, comfy seating, and acoustics that inspire creativity (or at least wicked loud noise).
  • Recording Studio: Hybrid analog/digital setup optimized for weird masterpieces, ambient soundscapes, or your next EP.

Instruments Available:

  • Guitars: Gibson Les Paul, Guild Dreadnaught 6-string, D'Angelico 12-string, Fender P-Bass 4-string, Classical 6-string nylon
  • Keyboards: 1916 Steinway Model O, Hammond B3 + M3 (both with Leslies), Roland FP-7, Korg Triton, Numa Compact X SE, and thousands of plugins
  • Drums: Gretsch Catalina Maple 4-piece (22"), Zildjian A Customs

Recording Gear:

  • Mics: 4x Shure SM57/58, 1x Beta52, 2x MXL 990/991, 3x sE X1 LDCs, 6x sE8 SDCs
  • Monitors: Custom built with love - and a few solder burns
  • Software: ProTools Ultimate, Logic Pro, iZotope Suite, Soundtoys, Slate Digital Pass, SSL Collection, PianoTeq 8, Kontakt, SynthMaster, Arturia V Collection, Steven Slate Trigger, and dozens of homemade samples/libraries

We're always happy to work with artists of all backgrounds. Whether you're a jazz trio, noise collective, or lo-fi beatmaker, this space is for you. Come plug in. Coming Soon:48 3"/6" acoustic panels, fully adjustable. And hopefully one day, a new location that isn't my parent's basement!
